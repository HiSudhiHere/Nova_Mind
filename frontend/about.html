About NovaMind

NovaMind is an AI–powered learning companion designed to transform the way students learn.

Upload your textbook pages, class notes, or syllabus, and NovaMind extracts the text and automatically generates structured topic-wise notes.

You can then ask questions, receive AI-powered explanations, and soon talk to NovaMind as if you are in a real classroom.

The goal is to make studying intuitive, visual, and interactive.
